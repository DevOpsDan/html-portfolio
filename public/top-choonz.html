Krazy G's top 3 choonz

Pianos, strings, composition. MJ Cole's production was perfect for this chart success.

Soulful meaningful vocals from Lain, laced with Wookie's catchy beats - a real gem


DJ Zinc - 138 Trek

An absolute dancefloor jammer, Zinc production hyping any dancefloor each and every time!
